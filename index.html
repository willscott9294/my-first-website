Our Process

At A&R HOMECRAFT, we make every remodeling project smooth, transparent, and tailored to your needs. Here's how we work: 1. Initial Consultation You call us to schedule an on-site consultation. We visit your spaceto discuss your goals, measure the area, and take photographs to capture every detail. 2. Design & Planning Using the information gathered, we begin designing your space. Our goal is to create a functional and beautiful plan that matches your vision. 3. Quote & Follow-Up Once the design is complete, we prepare a detailed project quote. We then schedule a follow-up appointment to review the design concept and cost, ensuring you understand every aspect of the project before moving forward. 4. Project Scheduling & Material Procurement After the project is contracted, we order all necessary materials and schedule the work. Your project begins as soon as all materials are on-site, ensuring an efficient and timely process.
